My name is Marcin Maslanka. I've been learning front end development for 6 months, mostly - by myself. However, I took part in one month front-end bootcamp by Coder's Lab, where I was able to brush up my skills and gain knowledge about new technologies. I am a highly motivated quick learner and hard working person, who loves crafting beautiful websites.
